**OneFlow** is a modular Project Management system designed to solve a critical real-world business challenge:  
the **fragmentation between project execution and financial tracking**.

Project managers and finance teams often rely on separate tools for:

- **Planning:** Tasks, timelines, and resource allocation.  
- **Execution:** Time logging, status updates, and blocker tracking.  
- **Finance/Billing:** Creating Sales Orders (revenue), recording Purchase Orders (costs), generating Customer Invoices, and managing Expenses.

This separation leads to **delayed billing**, **inaccurate profitability analysis**, and **wasted time reconciling data**.  
Our goal is to **unify this entire workflow**.
